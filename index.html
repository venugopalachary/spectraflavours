About Us

Welcome To Spectra Flavours

Spectra Flavours is a flavour manufacturing company. With more than 20 years’ experience in ever changing and highly competitive industry, we pride ourselves on being flexible, innovative and efficient which allows us to react to and prepare for any eventuality that we or our customers might encounter. Our product quality, customer support and employees are of the utmost importance. We firmly believe that without the highest quality products, satisfied customers and inspired employees it would be nearly impossible to achieve our goals of true service excellence in all we do from development to production.

We Offer A Variety Of Products When Quality Matters To You And Your Customers

Beverages

Flavor plays a key role in creating differentiated beverages consumers will love. Our experts can enhance your beverages with creative flavor combinations and high quality traditional favorites.

Confectionery

Our seasoned team of formulators can help you produce delicious and trend-leading confectionery products with real customer appeal.

Nutrition

Nutrition and great taste do not always go hand in hand. Our specialized nutritional expertise – drawn from our parent company, Carbery – and flavor creation technology, mean that now they can.
